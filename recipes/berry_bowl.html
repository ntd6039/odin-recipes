Berry bowl with honey

Home

A great desert for those who enjoy the simpler things in life. Easy to prepare and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 75g of frozen blue berries
  • 65g of fresh strawberries
  • 110 lb of frozen raspberries
  • 21g of honey
  • 25g of cool whip

    Steps

    1. Wash and cut into halves your strawberries.
    2. Mix your honey and cool whip in a small bowl.
    3. Combine the berries.
    4. Add the whipped honey mixture on top.
    5. Serve and enjoy.
