Gojujang Chicken thighs

Home

A great desert for those who enjoy the simpler things in life. Easy to prepare and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 600g of boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 50g of seseame oil
  • 200g of gojujang
  • 250ml of rice vinegar
  • 100g of honey

    Steps

    1. Wash and prep chicken.
    2. Mix seseame oil, gojujang, rice vinegar, and honey and rub onto cleaned chicken and marinate.
    3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes. 24 hours ideally.
    4. Sear the chicken breast and brown on one side for 5 minutes on high heat.
    5. Cook over medium heat for 8-12 minute or until cooked throughly.
